Mapo Tofu and Pork

Description

This is a spicy and savory dish originating in the Sichuan region of China.

Ingredients

  • Chicken broth
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Sambal Olek paste
  • Tofu
  • Ground pork
  • Water chestnuts
  • Bok choy
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Combine the first four ingredients to make the sauce
  2. Brown the ground pork in oil
  3. Add the water chestnuts
  4. Add the sauce and bring to a simmer
  5. Add the tofu and continue simmering for 3-5 min
  6. Turn off heat, add chopped bok choy
  7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve immediately
